About Istana Bandar

Istana Bandar, which is better known as the Palace with 40 Rooms was the official residence of the 5th Sultan of Selangor, Almarhum Sultan Alaeddin Sulaiman Shah Ibni Almarhum Raja Muda Musa (ruled 1898-1938).
